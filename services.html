Financial Services

Veracity combines decades of finance transition and transformation experience, to create value for SMEs, Accounting Firms and Shared Services/GBS. We aim to deliver lower costs, improve efficiencies, and higher quality by leveraging technology and talent. Our abundant pool of experienced professionals deliver work across the entire spectrum of the finance function, from bookkeeping to financial reporting. Furthermore, given the accelerating pace of change of technology and the ensuing disruption, we can help you devise the right digital strategy so that your organisation can thrive and be more agile in the digital age.
